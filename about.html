ABOUT THE   SOLELY FOOD

 We Have The Largest And Loved Food Chain

The Solely Food is the world's largest and loved food chain, directly under the ITEC group. The Solely Food is proud to be present in Vietnam since March 2021. This event marks a significant milestone to demonstrate the brand's commitment to premium food quality and professional service.

 Development

The Solely Food has been present in Vietnam since 2021 with 100% foreign capital and has now developed more than 20 restaurants with over 1,000 employees. The Solely Food Vietnam-where you can unleash yourself with fascinating opportunities for both personal and professional development!

 Food Safety

Food safety is a top priority at The Solely Food. The Solely Food strongly commits that all products are strictly con-trolled from ingredients supplies to food processing and customer serving process. Customers would be completely assured that The Solely Food products are best guaranteed due to the following reasons:

- Made from selected ingredients following food safety standards and the highest quality standards set USA.

- Ensured nutrition.

- Raw materials are strictly controlled from the selection of suppliers, receipt and preservations of ingredients and food processing in compliance with the strict standards of ITEC, which areperiodically assessed to ensure eﬀective management and control.
